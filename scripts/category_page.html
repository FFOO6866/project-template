Looking for the best safety shoes or safety boots for work? It is important to choose the right pair of safety shoes or safety boots to protect your hands in any work environment. Browse our selection today for high-quality safety shoes or safety boots at great prices.

Horme Hardware carries an enormous variety of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) for foot protection products, ranging from low-cut safety shoes, mid-cut safety shoes, high-cut safety shoes, ladies safety shoes, and lightweight safety shoes. Kitchen safety shoes, which provide a slip-resistant feature, are also available.

The Ministry of Manpower (MOM) plays a pivotal role by mandating the use of appropriate safety shoes, particularly in high-risk occupations. MOM-approved safety shoe standards draw from internationally recognized benchmarks, emphasizing the commitment to upholding the highest safety standards.

To qualify as safety shoes, the shoes need to meet one of the following standards:
Singapore standard SS513
European standard ISO 20345
American standard F2413
Japanese standard JIS T8101
Australian standard AS 2210.3

All safety shoes have slip-resistant features, and they can be further classified as steel-toe boots, composite safety shoes, or nanocarbon safety shoes depending on their properties. With no metal parts, composite safety shoes can be worn through metal detectors.

At Horme Hardware, we carry the best lightweight safety shoes in Singapore. The Safety Jogger Lightweight Safety Shoes Series is made of nanocarbon material, making it the lightest safety shoe on the market.

ESD safety shoes conduct static electricity through the insole, linings, cement, outsole, and into the ground, helping regulate the buildup of electrical charge on a persons body. Electrical hazard (EH)-rated safety shoes and boots are a must for anyone who works in environments where the potential for electrical shock or electrocution could be a high risk. HRO safety shoes have an outsole that can resist high temperatures up to 300 C, making them a must for anyone who works in high-temperature environments.
